01
Discover

We embed into your world before we open Figma or write a line of code. Every engagement begins with a deep audit of your market, your competitors, your current brand and product, and most importantly — your unfair advantage.

This phase produces the strategic foundation everything else is built on. Clarity first. Motion second.

Duration: 1–2 weeks

02
Structure

Architecture before aesthetics. We define the system: positioning, narrative, product hierarchy, information architecture, growth model, and the technical approach. Stakeholder alignment happens here, not midway through a build.

Every decision downstream refers back to the structure we agree on in this phase. This is what eliminates the expensive rework that plagues most engagements.

Duration: 1–2 weeks

03
Build

Brand, product, web, and automation — built in parallel by one integrated team. No handoffs, no lost context, no two agencies disagreeing about the brief. Just velocity with craft.

We work in two-week sprints with regular previews. You see work in progress, not just deliverables. Feedback loops are tight and intentional.

Duration: 4–10 weeks depending on scope

04
Launch

Sequenced GTM execution built around your market's specific dynamics. PR coordination, paid acquisition, organic seeding, referral engine activation — all firing at the right moment in the right order.

We've run enough launches to know the difference between a big launch and a loud one. We aim for the former.

Duration: 1–2 weeks

05
Scale

Post-launch is where compounding begins. We stay in the room — iterating on what's working, automating what isn't, and building infrastructure for the next stage of growth.

Most clients stay with us in a retainer capacity after launch. The relationship tends to deepen as we accumulate context about your business.

Ongoing: Monthly retainers from $8K/mo

Common questions
How long does a typical engagement take?+
Most full-stack launch engagements run 8–14 weeks from kickoff to launch. Focused engagements (brand only, web only) are typically 4–6 weeks. We'll scope precisely once we understand your needs.
Do you work with pre-launch or post-launch startups?+
Both. Pre-launch clients typically want the full stack — brand, product, and GTM together. Post-launch clients often come to us for specific capabilities: a rebrand, a conversion lift, or AI automation.
What's the typical investment?+
Full-stack launch engagements start at $45K. Focused capability engagements start at $18K. Ongoing retainers start at $8K/month. We'll give you a precise scope during our strategy call.
Can you work with our existing team?+
Absolutely. Many of our best engagements are collaborative — we embed alongside your internal design or engineering team, bringing capabilities and velocity that complement what you already have.
What happens if we need changes mid-build?+
Our process builds in structured feedback checkpoints precisely to avoid mid-build pivots. That said, real businesses evolve, and we're experienced at adapting. Material scope changes are handled transparently with revised timelines and costs.
